Digital Media Club First Meeting

31 August 2017

Today was the first general meeting of the digital media club. Although not everyone showed up due to a miscommunication regarding the time of the meeting, a lot of people were present. Mostly an informational meeting, but it seemed like people are as excited as I am for this coming year. Looking forward to all the awesome games, films, and photos the club will produce this year!

For next week, I'm going to host a programming workshop while a friend/mentor, Hunter Wu, is going to host a pixel animation workshop. Should be awesome. Going even further into the future, the cabinet and I need to prepare for Club Rush week. We're not quite sure what to present yet since we're a new club, but we should have something if we're going to recruit more members.
